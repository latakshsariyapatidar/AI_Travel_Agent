# Sample Conversations

Here are some sample conversations you can use to test the AI Travel Agent.

## Scenario 1: General Destination Planning

**User Input:**
> "I want to plan a 5-day trip to Kyoto, Japan next spring. I'm interested in cultural sites, good food, and would like a mix of popular attractions and hidden gems."

**Expected Output:**
The AI should provide a structured 5-day itinerary tailored to the prompt. It should include:
*   **Cultural Sites:** Suggestions like Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Fushimi Inari Taisha, and Kiyomizu-dera.
*   **Food:** Recommendations for local specialties like Kaiseki, Matcha treats, and street food at Nishiki Market.
*   **Hidden Gems:** Less crowded temples like Otagi Nenbutsu-ji or Gio-ji.
*   **Logistics:** Mentioning that spring is Sakura (cherry blossom) season, so booking accommodations in advance is crucial.

---

## Scenario 2: Budget-Conscious Weekend Getaway

**User Input:**
> "Can you suggest a cheap weekend getaway from New York City for under $300 per person? We like hiking and nature."

**Expected Output:**
The AI should suggest affordable nature-focused options easily accessible from NYC.
*   **Destinations:** Examples like the Catskill Mountains, Hudson Valley, or Delaware Water Gap.
*   **Activities:** Specific hiking trails or nature walks suitable for a weekend.
*   **Budget Guidance:** Tips on how to keep costs under the $300 limit (e.g., affordable lodging, packing lunches).
*   **Transport:** Commuting options like taking the Metro-North train or renting a car.

---

## Scenario 3: Specific Constraints (Dietary & Niche Interests)

**User Input:**
> "I'm traveling to Rome for a week. I'm vegan and love art history. What are the best places to eat, and what lesser-known museums should I visit besides the Vatican and Colosseum?"

**Expected Output:**
The AI should strictly adhere to the user's specific constraints and bypass the generic tourist spots mentioned.
*   **Vegan Food in Rome:** Recommendations for vegan-friendly Italian restaurants or traditional dishes that are naturally vegan (e.g., Pizza Marinara, Pasta e Ceci).
*   **Lesser-Known Art Museums:** Suggestions like Galleria Doria Pamphilj, Palazzo Massimo alle Terme, or Villa Farnesina.
*   **Context:** Brief explanations of why these specific museums and galleries are significant for an art history enthusiast.
